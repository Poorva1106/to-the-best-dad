What I Want You To Know

Watching you become a father has been one of the most beautiful things I have ever witnessed...

Happy Father's Day ❤️ Six months ago, our lives changed forever. I knew you would be a good father, but I never knew how much I would admire you once I saw you become one.
Thank you for every sleepless night, every diaper change, every walk around the room when our little one wouldn't sleep, and every time you stepped in when I was exhausted. Thank you for being patient on the difficult days and present during the beautiful ones. Some of my favorite memories aren't the big milestones. They're the quiet moments—watching you hold him close when he falls asleep on your shoulder, hearing you talk to him in your silly voice, seeing the smile on your face when he recognizes you after a long day. This year has not been easy. We've both been tired, overwhelmed, and learning as we go. But through it all, I've never had to wonder whether our baby is loved. The way you look at him answers that question every single day. Our little boy may not remember these first six months, but one day he'll know how deeply he was loved from the very beginning. He'll know that his father showed up for him every day, even when he was tired. He'll know that his father made him feel safe, cherished, and important. And I hope you know this too: while our baby got a wonderful father, I got to see a side of you that made me fall in love with you all over again. Thank you for being our safe place, our teammate, and the best dad our little one could ever ask for. We love you more than words can say. Happy Father's Day.
Love, Purva & Avadhut❤️
